Our Services

Just Smart Business is your trusted partner in business strategic advisory services, small business growth strategies, and entrepreneurial mentoring.

Strategic Advisory

Success is achieved through goal achievement. We assist you to create your 'Big Goal' and a Strategic Purpose for your business. This is supported by a simple Scorecard and Action Plan that ensures you focus on the key issues that will drive your business forward.

We utilise a modified 'Balanced Scorecard' approach which provides a simple, one-page strategy that is easy to communicate and drives action. This powerful approach has been successfully used by thousands of companies worldwide to achieve clarity and alignment.

Key Benefits:

  • A clear, one-page strategic plan.
  • Identified priorities and key performance indicators.
  • Actionable steps to achieve your 'Big Goal'.
  • Improved decision-making and resource allocation.

What You Get:

  • Regular, structured accountability meetings.
  • Expert guidance to navigate business challenges.
  • Support to maintain focus and momentum.
  • An experienced co-pilot on your business journey.

Entrepreneurial Mentoring

A great strategy is only effective if it's implemented. We act as your accountability partner to keep you focused and on track. Through regular, structured meetings, we assist you in implementing your strategy, reviewing your progress, and adapting to new challenges and opportunities.

This ongoing support provides the discipline and external perspective needed for effective execution, helping you navigate the path to success with confidence.
