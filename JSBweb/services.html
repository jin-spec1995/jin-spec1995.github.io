Need to Know: Service FAQs

How are your advisory engagements structured? ▼

Every engagement starts with a strategy sprint that delivers your strategic plan, scorecard, and cash flow model. From there we set an accountability rhythm that suits your business rhythm best.

Can you work alongside our existing accountant? ▼

Yes! We regularly collaborate with accountants, bookkeepers, marketing partners, and internal leaders. Our role is to connect the strategic plan to service delivery, so everyone rows in the same direction.

Do you offer in-person sessions or virtual support? ▼

Yes. We meet onsite across Brisbane and South East Queensland and combine that with online sessions through the JSB Platform so momentum continues wherever your team is based.
