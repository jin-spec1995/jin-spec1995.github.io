Need to Know: Service FAQs

Answers to the questions Brisbane owners ask when exploring advisory support.

How are your advisory engagements structured?

Every engagement starts with a 4-6 week strategy sprint that delivers your one-page plan, scorecard, and cash flow model. From there we set a fortnightly or monthly rhythm with dashboards, actions, and optional project support.

Can you work alongside our existing accountant or coach?

Absolutely. We regularly collaborate with accountants, bookkeepers, marketing partners, and internal leaders. Our role is to connect the strategic plan to delivery so everyone rows in the same direction.

Do you offer in-person sessions or virtual support?

Yes. We frequently meet onsite across Brisbane and South East Queensland, and we combine that with video strategy sessions, real-time dashboards, and resources inside the JSB Platform so your team can engage from anywhere.
