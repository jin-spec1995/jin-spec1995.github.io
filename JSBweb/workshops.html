Our Business Growth Workshops

Our hands-on workshops are designed to give you practical tools and a clear plan. You’ll walk away with tangible assets for your business, not just theory.

The Big Picture – Strategic Planning

This foundational workshop helps you define your long-term vision and create a clear, actionable roadmap to get there.

  • ✓Define your 'Big Goal' and articulate your Strategic Purpose.
  • ✓Identify and prioritise the key issues holding your business back.
  • ✓Walk away with a one-page strategic plan to guide your decisions.

Show Me the Money – Financial Planning

Gain control of your finances. This workshop demystifies financial forecasting and gives you the tools to manage cash flow effectively.

  • ✓Develop simple 3-year financial projections for your business.
  • ✓Create a simple scorecard to track your most important financial goals.
  • ✓Understand the key drivers of profit and cash flow in your business.

InvestReady Program

Prepare your business for its next stage of growth by becoming 'investor-ready'. We guide you through the process of securing capital.

  • ✓Understand what investors are looking for and how to prepare.
  • ✓Develop a compelling pitch deck and financial model.
  • ✓Gain confidence in presenting your business and its valuation.

Marketing & Sales Strategy

Turn your marketing efforts into measurable results. This workshop helps you build a practical strategy to attract and convert your ideal customers.

  • ✓Clearly define your target audience and unique value proposition.
  • ✓Create a simple yet effective sales and marketing funnel.
  • ✓Develop a plan to generate consistent leads and grow revenue.
