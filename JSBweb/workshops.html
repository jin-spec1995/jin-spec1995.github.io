Workshops & Programs

Hands-on, practical sessions designed to deliver tangible results and actionable plans for your business.

Big Picture Workshop

This foundational workshop is designed to give you ultimate clarity on your business direction. We work with you to define your 'Big Goal', articulate a compelling Strategic Purpose, identify and prioritise the key issues holding you back, and set actionable goals to overcome them. You'll leave with the cornerstone of your business strategy, ready for implementation.

Show Me the Money Workshop

Gain complete control over your business finances. In this session, we help you create comprehensive 3-year financial projections, including Profit & Loss, Cash Flow, and Balance Sheet. You'll also learn how to use a simple scorecard to monitor your financial health, enabling you to make informed decisions and drive profitability.

Marketing Workshop

Stop wasting money on ineffective marketing. This workshop focuses on defining your ideal target customer, developing a powerful value proposition that resonates with them, and creating a focused marketing action plan. We ensure that every dollar you spend on marketing is a strategic investment towards customer acquisition and growth.

InvestReady Program

If you're seeking external funding, this program is essential. We guide you through the process of preparing your business for investment, which includes creating a compelling and professional Information Memorandum. We then help you connect with our extensive network of venture capitalists and angel investors to get your pitch in front of the right people.
