Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last Updated: 9 October 2025

1. Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Just Smart Business ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal information in accordance with the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) contained within the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth). This policy applies to information we collect through our website (justsmartbusiness.com.au) and in the course of providing our services.

2. What Information We Collect

We may collect personal information from you when you interact with us, such as when you fill out our contact form, book a session, or engage our services. The types of personal information we may collect include:

  • Your name
  • Your contact details (e.g., email address, phone number)
  • Information about your business or inquiry
  • Any other information you voluntarily provide to us

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the purposes for which it was collected, which include:

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide you with our consulting and coaching services.
  • To communicate with you about our services, workshops, and events.
  • To process transactions and maintain our business records.
  • To improve our website and services.
  • To comply with our legal obligations.

We will not use your personal information for any other purpose without your consent.

4. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from misuse, loss, unauthorised access, modification, or disclosure. Your information may be stored in secure physical and electronic formats. We retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

5. Use of Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us understand user behaviour, such as which pages are most visited. This information is aggregated and anonymous and helps us improve our website. You can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies, but this may affect the functionality of our site.

6. Sharing of Your Information

We do not sell or trade your personal information. We may disclose your information to third parties only in the following circumstances:

  • To our trusted partners (as listed on our About Us page) but only with your explicit consent to facilitate a referral or integrated service.
  • To our third-party service providers who assist us in operating our business (e.g., payment processors, IT services), under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • If required to do so by law or in response to a valid legal request.

7. Your Rights and Access to Information

You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you and to request its correction. To do so, please contact us using the details below. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review it periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
