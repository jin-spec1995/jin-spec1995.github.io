Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions we receive from Brisbane founders and leadership teams who are exploring advisory support.

What types of businesses does Just Smart Business support?

We work with Queensland-based businesses across professional services, trades, technology, health, and community sectors. If you have a team of two or more and are committed to implementing change, we can tailor strategic support to suit your stage.

How does an advisory engagement typically run?

We start with a diagnostic workshop to map goals, financial position, and current challenges. From there we co-design a roadmap with milestones, fortnightly or monthly accountability sessions, and on-demand support between meetings to keep delivery on track.

Do you offer in-person sessions or virtual support?

Yes. We frequently meet onsite across Brisbane and South East Queensland, and we combine that with video strategy sessions, real-time dashboards, and resources inside the JSB Platform so your team can engage from anywhere.
